Dr. Bruno Woltzenlogel Paleo has a PhD degree in Computational Logic and Formal Methods. He has been Director of Engineering for Cardano. He is the main author of formally verified Djed and Gluon stablecoin protocols, an editor of the Journal of Financial Technology and the invited author of the entry about stablecoins in the Encyclopedia of Cryptography, Security and Privacy.
